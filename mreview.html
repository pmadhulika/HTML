Movie Review

Hello Meera

Release Date : April 21, 2023

Rating : 3.5/5

Cast: Gargeyi Yellapragada

Director: Srinivasu Kakarla

Cinematography: Prashanth Koppineedi

Streaming Partner:Amazon Prime

Highlights

  • Meera is the only character seen on screen throughout the film, could be any young girl with hopes and dreams.

  • The minute detail regarding the characters on the other side was stunningly presented.

  • The entire film runs on phone conversations and we get to hear Meera's fiancee Kalyan,
    Meera's parents and in-laws, police officials, goons, tailor, and many other characters.
Plus Points
  • Gargeyi, as Meera, did an impressive job carrying the whole film on her shoulders, portraying the nuances of a girl
    who is about to get married the next day and as a worried soul who gets embroiled in a nasty police case.
  • The first half has some good moments, and the film moves at a decent pace during this hour.
  • The sound effects and the camera angles should be highly superior for a viewer to connect to the movie,Hello Meera gets it right in these aspects.

Minus Points

  • Most part of the story gets completed in the first half, and there is nothing much that happens in the second hour plotwise.
  • The thrill element, which was maintained in the first half, gets bogged down towards the climax.
